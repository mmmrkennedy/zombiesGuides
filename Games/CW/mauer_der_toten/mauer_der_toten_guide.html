CBRS-S

The CBRS-S is available through the mystery box, completion of challenges, and via the following quest:
The CBRS-S can simply be Pack-A-Punched and does not require an Easter Egg to do so.

  1. Assemble the base version of Klaus.
  2. Have Klaus open the Locker located in the Switch Control Room by using the Tactical Equipment to move him in front of it.
  3. Pick up the Blacklight from the Locker.
  4. In the following three locations there are 2 numbers, one visible with the blacklight (coloured blue/white) and one ranging from 1 to 3 visible with or without the blacklight (coloured black):
    • Garment Factory (Black Number):
      • Chalkboard
      • Doorway
      • Graffiti
    • Service Passage (Black Number):
      • Doorway
      • Pipes
      • Electrical Box
    • Grocery Store (Black Number):
      • Mugs Poster
      • Shelves
      • Watermelon Poster
  5. After finding all 6 numbers, go to Hotel Room 305 and interact with the safe. The black numbers represent the dial and the blue number is the number the dial needs to be set to.
  6. Once you enter the right combination, the safe will open revealing the CBRS-S ready for pickup.

Variants

    Swarm, Blazer or Diffuser

    All the variants can be obtained from killing Zombies. The is no way to get a specific variant to drop, you just simply have to wait for one to drop.

'Tin Man Heart' Easter Egg Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 5/10, Est Completion Time - 40 Mins to 1 Hr, RNG Rating - 0/5

    Prerequisites

  1. Turn on Power and Open Pack-a-Punch
  2. Assemble and Fully Upgrade Klaus
  3. Obtain the CBRS-S

    4. Find the Secret Lab

  4. You must craft the Hacking Helm. There are three items, two of which are only on one spot. The object must be shot with the CBRS-S to make them drop the parts. Locations are:
    • Apartment Rooftop
    • Ghost Station
    • Electronics Store - Location 1, Location 2, Location 3
  5. Once all three items have been collected, navigate to the Safe House and interact with Klaus to craft the Hacking Helm.
  6. Navigate to Sewer Access and use the Klaus Remote Control to Command Klaus to break the brick wall to reveal the metal wall.
  7. Obtain the Blazer Mod Kit (Laser Beam Variant) and shoot the Metal Wall till it melts.

    8. Charge the Conversion Unit

  8. Once you enter the lab the current round will end and a Tormentor round will begin. Simply finish the round.
  9. Collect the three empty canisters from the Conversion Unit.
  10. Shoot this machine with the CBRS-S to open it. Once open, you are able to pick up an Endstation Lure.
  11. Locate three Essence Harvesters that are lit up green and interact with it to insert the Empty Canisters:
    • West Berlin Street
    • Destroyed Penthouse
    • East Berlin Street
    • Korber Rooftop
    • Alley
    • Sewer Access
    • Ghost Station
  12. Once one of the Canisters has been inserted, throw the Lure on top/near the Essence Harvesters. You will have done it correctly if Tempests start to spawn. If Tempests don't spawn simply try again with another trap.
  13. Kill the tempests to charge the canister. Once the Tempests have been killed interact with the Essence Harvesters to pick up the canisters. If you can't pick up the canister simply try again with another trap.
  14. Bring the filled canister to Conversion Unit and deposit it.
  15. Repeat Step 11-14 for the other two Canisters.

    16. Stop the Train

  16. Navigate to the Switch Control Room and interact with the Railway Switch.
  17. Command Klaus to stand on the track furthest from Mule Kick. If done correctly, Klaus will stop the train allowing you board it. If Klaus fails to stop the train, you have to wait for next round to flip the Railway Switch and try again.
  18. On the train, you must pick up Rico's Keycard and the Bomb before the train leaves.
  19. Return to the Secret Lab and interact with the Conversion Unit to place the Bomb.

    20. Obtain the Uranium

  20. Navigate to the Safe House and interact with this computer until the screen shows a satellite. Navigate to the Objective Marker and kill the HVT Megaton.
  21. This will drop two Uranium Rocks, bring one of them to the nearest Workbench, locations below, to craft a Uranium Device.
    • West Berlin Street
    • Military Tent
  22. Navigate to the Destroyed Penthouse Zipline and attach the Uranium Device.
  23. Repeat Step 21 for the other Radioactive Material and then navigate to Korber Rooftop Zipline and attach the Uranium Device.
  24. Both Uranium Devices will shoot across the zipline, collide in the middle and land on the street below. Collect the Cleansed Uranium and return it to the Conversion Unit.

    25. Warning: Point of No Return. Once you do the next step, you will be put into the Boss Fight

  25. Repeat Steps 20-24 to get another Cleansed Uranium.

    26. Valentina Boss Fight:

  26. Valentina has three attacks, two damage dealing and one healing. They are:
    • Fire (Damage): Three Orange crystals will appear over Valentina's Head and begin shooting at a player. The projectiles can be outrun but can not be avoided while walking.
    • Wipe (Damage): A blue aura will appear around your screen. All players must take cover to avoid being instantly downed.
    • Heal: Valentina will spawn floating zombies surrounding her. Kill the zombies to stop her from healing.
  27. Simply shoot Valentina till she becomes Immune and move to a new area. She will move four times ending back at the secret lab.
  28. Once Valentina is dead, a cutscene will play then you must defend Klaus as he walks the Bomb into the Portal.
  29. Once he makes it into the portal, the game will end and cutscene will start.

Klaus

    Assembling Klaus

  1. Turn on Power and Unlock Pack-a-Punch
  2. Equip a weapon with Brain Rot
  3. Navigate to Hotel Room 305 and spawn a Brain Rotted Zombie near the Barricaded Door.
  4. The zombie will tear down the barricade, allowing you to access the room. Once in the room pickup the Robotic Hands from the corpse on the bed.
  5. Kill a Krasny Soldat and Pick Up the Battery. They spawn in for the first time around round 10.
  6. Navigate to the Safe House and interact with Klaus to install the Hands and Battery.

    Klaus Upgrade

  1. Navigate to "Death Strip", and interact with all the Debris Piles until you receive a Microwave Dish.
  2. Navigate to the Garment Factory and install the Dish on the Upgrade Station.
  3. Deploy Klaus and allow him to kill about 45 Zombies. Once he gets enough kills a sound will play and the monitor on the top-right of the Upgrade Station will turn green.
  4. Command Klaus to enter the Upgrade Station. You must defend Klaus from infinitely spawning enemies until the timer finishes counting down.
  5. Have Klaus open the Locker located in the Switch Control Room by using the Tactical Equipment to move him in front of it. Pick up the Blacklight from it.
  6. Shine the Blacklight at each of the following electrical boxes, two of which will have a Klaus Symbol on it.
  7. Command Klaus to the two boxes to open them revealing floppy disks.
    • 5th Floor Apartments: Across from Zipline
    • Hotel Lounge: Across from Stamin-Up
    • Ghost Station: Near Service Passage
    • Maintenance Tunnel: Near Safe House
    • West Berlin Street: Near Café Müller
    • Blasted Suite: Near Barrier
  8. Collect the Floppy Disks and return to the Upgrade Station
  9. Command Klaus to enter the Upgrade Station and interact with it to insert one of the disks and begin the Upgrade Process. You will not have to defend Klaus during the Floppy Disk upgrades.
  10. Repeat for the other Floppy Disk.
  11. Klaus is now fully upgraded.
