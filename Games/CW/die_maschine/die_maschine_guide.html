D.I.E. Shockwave

The D.I.E. Shockwave is available through the mystery box, completion of challenges, and via the following quest:

  1. Kill a Megaton and Pick Up the Keycard.
  2. Go to the Weapons Lab (Deadshot Room) and interact with the Weapon Locker and pick Up the D.I.E. Remote Control.
  3. Go to the "Living Room" (Ground Floor of Nacht) and interact with this Broken Wall to activate the D.I.E. It will now suck up zombies through the door.
  4. Once enough zombies are sucked up, interact with the wall again, and the D.I.E. will shoot the door off.
  5. The D.I.E. is available for pickup.

Upgrades

  • Nova-5
    1. Go to the "Mezzanine" and find the Empty Canister outside the map. Pull the canister toward you with the Alternate fire mode on the D.I.E.
    2. Pick up the Canister and bring it to the "Weapons Lab". Find the Cleaning Unit and place the Canister in it.
    3. Kill a Plaguehound near the canister
    4. Pick up the canister and place it on the Nova-5 Weapon Crate. Melee the canister.
    5. You can now pick up the Nova-5 upgrade from the Weapon Crate.
  • Cryo-Emitter
    1. Go to the "Penthouse" and find the wooden crate on the ledge of the building and shoot it with the D.I.E. to knock it down. You can pick up the Flask from the crate.
    2. Wait for a Megaton to spawn and bring it to the tree in the "Pond" with Fungus growing off it.
    3. Have the Megaton shoot the Fungus with his long range attack. If the Fungus is hit, it will turn blue
    4. Place the Flask below the Fungus on the ground. Wait 60 Seconds for the flask to fill.
    5. Once filled, pick up the flask and interact with the Weapon Crate in the "Medical Bay"
    6. You can now pick up the Cryo-Emitter upgrade from the Weapon Crate.
  • Elecrobolt
    1. Progress through the Main Quest until you have entered the password into the computer in the "Medical Bay."
    2. Enter the Anomaly on the lowest level of the Particle Accelerator
    3. Locate 3 Glowing Crystals and use the alt fire mode on the D.I.E. to collect the energy
      • The Crash Site: Left of Juggernog
      • "Penthouse": Right of Wunderfizz
      • Pond: Right of Mystery Box
    4. Shoot the energy at The Weapon Crate in the Particle Accelerator
    5. Once all 3 have been collected and shot, you can pick up the upgrade from the Weapon Crate
  • Thermophasic
    1. Progress through the Main Quest until you have entered the password into the computer in the "Medical Bay."
    2. Enter the Anomaly in the "Pond"
    3. Go to The Crash Site and shoot/melee open the Fuse Box beside the Fast Travel Portal and Pick up the Fuse
    4. Go to the "Weapons Lab" and interact with the Plasma Cutter
    5. Exit the Dark Aether and go to the "Pond". Interact with the Plasma Cutter to get the Thermophasic Upgrade.

Main Easter Egg (Seal the Deal)

  1. Turn on Power and Activate Pack-a-Punch
  2. Collect the 3 Aetherscope parts in the Dark Aether. The parts spawn one at a time in a random order
    • Decoder: Particle Accelerator: near the Aether Tunnel
    • Antenna: Yard: beneath the concrete staircase near Rampage Inducer
    • Radio: Crash Site: near the cockpit on top of the plane debris
  3. Once you collect all 3 parts, exit the Dark Aether and build the Aetherscope at the workbench below PAP in the Particle Accelerator.
  4. Enter the Dark Aether Anomaly in the "Medical Bay." Upstairs in the Medical Bay, Collect Dr. Vogal's Diary.
  5. You must interact with 3 Spectral Reflections in order. You must interact with one twice (activating the reflections then handing over the book) before moving to the next one. You don't need to wait for one to finish before talking to another.
    • Medical Bay: Behind Speed Cola
    • Control Room: In-between Two Challenge Podiums
    • Particle Accelerator: In front of the Aetherscope Workbench
  6. Go back to the computer where you picked up the diary and interact with it.
  7. You must now obtain all 4 D.I.E. Upgrades
  8. Once you have obtained all 4 upgrades, you must shoot coloured ports the corresponding D.I.E. Upgrade
    • Nova-5 : Green Port
    • Cryo-emitter : Blue Port
    • Electrobolt : Yellow Port
    • Thermophasic: Orange Port
  9. Enter the Dark Aether via the Anomaly in the Medical Bay and watch the Relection on the walkway above Speed Cola
  10. Once the Relection ends, and you exit the Dark Aether, collect the Dark Aether Wrench on the ground where the Relection was.
  11. Go to the Yard and interact with The Tank 3 times. A zombie will then pop out of the top of the tank, kill it and throw an explosive at the open hatch. The Tank will fire if the explosive was aimed correctly.
  12. Go to The Crash Site and Pick up The Decontamination Agent Carry it back to where you picked up the wrench in the Medical Bay and place it in the containment unit.
    Note: You can not fast travel or mantle with the Decontamination Agent
  13. Wait for a Megaton to Spawn. Split it by shooting it and then bring the two split Megatons under the large machine on the ceiling of the Medical Bay. They will each get sucked in one at a time.
  14. Once the two Bombers are in the machine, go back to the computer and interact with it. This will start a cutscene.
  15. Once the cutscene ends, go to the Omega Outpost (door near workbench coming from spawn) and enter The Dark Aether. Near the M1911 wallbuy, there will be a Spectral Reflection, interact with it.

    16. Warning: Point of No Return. Once you do the next step, you will be up into the "boss fight"

  16. Once the Spectral Reflection is done, on a table by the reflection pick up the Orlov Family Photo.
  17. You will be teleported to the Particle Accelerator, where Orlov will talk to a bit before running to one of his objectives.
  18. You Must protect Orlov from the zombies while he deactivates the Particle Accelerator. Orlov can't die, but he will stop working if there are zombies on him.
  19. There are 3 things Orlov has to do, the Magnetic Containment, Primary Coolant, and Fail-Safe.
  20. Once the Particle Accelerator is deactivated, a short dialogue will play, followed by a 90-second timer will start where the players need to make it thorough the facility avoiding lethal beams of electricity coming from the ground to get to the exfil chopper. There is only one path out which takes you through the Weapons Lab to the Control Room, out to the Tunnel then The Crash Site then down to the Pond. Board the exfil chopper to finish the Easter Egg.

'Alone' Song

  1. There are 3 tapes required to start the song
    • "Living Room": Inside the toppled refrigerator
    • Medical Bay: On a shelf near a wooden crate
    • Particle Accelerator: On top of machinery
  2. Once all 3 tapes are collected, the song will start

Coffin Dance (Jug EE)

  1. Turn on Power and Open Pack a Punch
  2. There are 5 orbs that need to be shot
    • Near the right-side door to Main Power
    • Within the barrier beneath the stairs
    • On an air duct near the door to Weapons Lab
    • Behind the grate on the Main Power wall
    • Behind the machinery to the right of the stairs
  3. Once all 5 orbs were shot, you will be teleported to the Dark Aether to watch a cutscene. Once the cutscene is over, you will be presented with a chest.
