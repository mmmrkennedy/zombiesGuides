Perk Statues

  • There are four Perk Statues, three of which change locations each game. One perk will always be on the 'Mausoleum', another in the 'Gardens' and the last either in the 'Wine Cellar' or 'Forest Terrace'. There is a way to easily tell which Statue is at the 'Mausoleum' and 'Gardens' by checking the top of the doorways to the Library and Dining Room. The Library corresponds to the Mausoleum and Dining Room to Gardens. Doorway Symbols:
    • Danu: Always in the Entrance Hall
    • Ra: Doorway Symbol
    • Zeus: Doorway Symbol
    • Odin: Doorway Symbol

    Chaos Theory

    The Chaos Theory is an enhanced version of Alistair's Folly that has two new attacks which can be triggered by holding down the trigger until the weapon is fully charged. The new attacks are a Tornado that picks up any Zombies that enter and a Yellow/Green Pool that turns Zombies friendly that walk into it.

  1. Obtain Silver Bullets.
  2. Kill a Werewolf with a weapon equipped with Silver Bullets. If done correctly, Werewolf Chaos Material should drop from the Werewolf.
  3. Place the Werewolf Chaos Material into the machinery in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory'. After a few seconds, the machine will produce Prima Materia to be picked up from the left side of the machine.
  4. Obtain the Ballistic Shield.
  5. Melee the bookshelf at the back left of the 'Library', right of the Silver Bullets buildable table, with the Ballistic Shield. The bookshelf should slide open revealing a gun part glowing blue.
  6. Obtain the Alistair's Folly.
  7. Craft the Chaos Theory at the buildable table left of the stairs in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory'.

    Alistair's Annihilator

    Alistair's Annihilator is an enhanced version of the Chaos Theory that has two new charged attacks, as well as the attacks the Chaos Theory had. The new attacks are a Red Orb that vaporizes Zombies that enter and a Green Pool that shrinks Zombies to death.

  1. Obtain the Chaos Theory.
  2. Use the Tornado attack from the Chaos Theory to kill vampires and make them drop Bile. Collect three Bile; after obtaining three, no more bile will drop.
  3. Interact with the red glowing coffin in the 'Cemetery' to smear the bile on the side. After a few seconds, a Crimson Nosferatu will spawn from it. Kill it to make it drop the Nosferatu Chaos Materia.
  4. In the 'Mausoleum', shoot the orange lamp with the Chaos Theory to switch it off and make another orange. After the first lamp is shot, quickly shoot the next two lamps, or they'll go out, requiring you to retry next round. Once all three lamps are shot, a bat spawn from the lamp and fly around the 'Cemetery' and 'Mausoleum'. Shoot it with the Chaos Theory to obtain the Bat Chaos Materia.
  5. In the 'Forest', there are three dirt piles with glowing mushrooms, with one hiding a gun part. When a zombie is near/on a pile, hit it with the Chaos Theory's green pool attack. If done right, the zombie will start digging. If that pile has the gun part, you can collect it; if not, repeat with the other piles until you find the part.
  6. Convert the Nosferatu Chaos Materia and Bat Chaos Materia into Prima Materia via the machinery in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory'. Note: Only one Materia can be converted at once.
  7. Craft the Alistair's Annihilator at the buildable table right of the stairs, near the ICR-7 wallbuy in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory'.

Silver Bullets

Silver Bullets are special ammunition that deals extra damage to werewolves. They can be equipped on any weapon (excluding wonder weapons and the shield), and more can be bought from the workbench at any time.

  1. There are six parts required for to craft the Silver Bullets, three to make molten silver and three to make gunpowder. The parts for the molten silver are in the following locations:
    • Candlestick:
      • Entrance Hall - On a table near the doorway
      • Billiard Room - Right of the fireplace, below a chair
      • Main Hall - Right of the fireplace, near the Mozu wallbuy
    • Vase:
      • West Hallway - Right of the couch, below the table
      • Dining Room - On the table opposite the clock
      • Dining Room - On the corner of the big table
    • Plate:
      • Wine Cellar - On the wall near the MX9 wallbuy
      • Wine Cellar - Opposite the workbench on a shelf
      • Wine Cellar - On the large wooden table, right of the noose.
  2. Craft the Molten Silver at the workbench in the 'Wine Cellar'.
  3. The parts for the gunpowder are in the following locations:
    • Guano:
      • Mausoleum - At the base of a tree to the right of the Mausoleum
      • Cemetery - Bottom of the left staircase leading up to the 'Mausoleum'
      • Cemetery - The part of the 'Cemetery' farthest from the 'Mausoleum', between the two entrances to the 'Cemetery'
    • Charcoal:
      • Master Bedroom - In the fireplace
      • Billiard Room - In the fireplace
      • Main Hall - In the fireplace left of the clock
    • Sulfur:
      • Greenhouse Laboratory - Left of the 'Greenhouse Laboratory' main entrance
      • Greenhouse Laboratory - Right of the 'Greenhouse Laboratory' main entrance
      • Greenhouse Laboratory - At the back of the greenhouse, near the ICR wallbuy and Alistair's Annihilator workbench
  4. Once all parts have been collected and the Molten Silver has been crafted, the Silver Bullets can be crafted at the workbench in the 'Library'.
