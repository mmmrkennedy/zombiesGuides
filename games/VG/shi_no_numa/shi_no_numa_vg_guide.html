Wunderwaffe DG-2

The Wunderwaffe DG-2 is available through the mystery box or via the following quest:
The Wunderwaffe DG-2 can simply be Pack-A-Punched and does not require an Easter Egg to do so.

  1. The Wunderwaffe requires four parts to build, the weapon barrel and three vacuum tubes.
  2. These parts can be obtained in any order.

    3. Weapon Barrel and First Vacuum Tube

  3. Navigate to the Fishing Hut and pick up the Weapon Barrel from a shelf near the door.
  4. Place the Weapon Barrel on the Radio Tower outside the Comm Room.
  5. At the start of the next round, a lightning bolt will strike the barrel and the Radio Tower will need to be defended.
  6. Once it's done, retrieve the Weapon Barrel from the Radio Tower and the charged Vacuum Tube from the radio in the Comm Room.

    7. Second Vacuum Tube

  7. Pick up the Electrical Fuse from the table in the Comm Room.
  8. Navigate to the Storage Hut and activate the trap. After being on for a while, the trap will short out and the red box will pop open.
  9. Interact with the red box to insert the new fuse to start the trap again.
  10. Allow the trap to kill zombies until the tube on the workbench lights up.

    11. Third Vacuum Tube

  11. Wait for Zaballa to spawn on round 15.
  12. Lure her to the Tesla Coil in the Excavation Room.
  13. Bait her into attacking the Tesla Coil with her energy mines by standing near the coil.
  14. Once the Coil has been hit five times, pick up the vacuum tube from the table beside the coil.

    15. Crafting the Wunderwaffe DG-2

  15. Wait for Zaballa to spawn on round 15.
  16. Navigate to the Storage Hut and craft the Wunderwaffe DG-2 at the Workbench.

Main Easter Egg Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 3/10, Est Completion Time - 25 to 40 Mins, RNG Rating - 0/5

    Note: The Wunderwaffe DG-2 is required for the Easter Egg, but not until Step 9

    The Monolith & The Ceremony

  1. Navigate to 'Doctor's Quarter's Exterior' to find the Monolith covered in vines.
  2. Explode a Boom Schreier next to the Monolith to clear the vines.
  3. Collect three Cypher Wheel parts:
    • On a table in the Dormitory
    • In the room right of the Demonic Fountain at the Doctor's Quarters
    • On a table at the Dig Site in front of the Pack-a-Punch.
  4. Interact with the Monolith to place the Cypher Wheels on it.
  5. Three symbols need to be found and translated to be input into the Monolith. The locations are:
    • On a table in the Excavation Room, the paper can be hidden beneath destructible books.
    • On the workbench in the Dig Site.
    • On a table in the Comm Room.

    Note: Step 6 can be skipped by using the graphic in step 7

  6. Inside the Doctor's Quarters, throw a molotov or Thermite on this piece of paper. This will reveal all the symbols on the paper.
  7. Using either this graphic or the piece of paper from Step 6, translate the symbols from step 5 into symbols that can be input into the Monolith.
  8. Input the Ã¯translated symbols into the Monolith. Doing so correctly will spawn a glowing stones near the edges of the area, one for each player in the game.
  9. Obtain the Wunderwaffe DG-2
  10. All players need to interact with a stone to "Start the Ceremony" which will trigger a lockdown.
  11. During the lockdown, zombies shrouded in a blue mist will spawn that need to be killed near the shrine with the Wunderwaffe DG-2 before the lockdown ends.
  12. If completed successfully, a blue orb will spawn and a conversation will start. If failed, simply try again next round.

    13. Repairing The Mirror

  13. Go to the Flogger Courtyard and fill the empty Demonic Fountain with blood by flinging zombies into it with the Flogger.
  14. Once filled enough, the player can drink from the fountain to obtain "Zombie Blood" view (Unlike previous maps, the zombies can still see and attack the player).
  15. While in "Zombie Blood", check the map in the War Room for where the red 'X' is.
  16. Go to the hut the red 'X' indicated and look in the ceiling to find a mirror piece.
  17. Shoot the mirror piece to make it fall to the floor and pick it up.
