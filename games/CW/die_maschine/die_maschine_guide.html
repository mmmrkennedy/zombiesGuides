D.I.E. Shockwave

The D.I.E. Shockwave can be Pack-a-Punch'd and does not require an Easter Egg to do so. Also, the D.I.E. Shockwave can be converted in the Nova-5, Cryo Emitter, Electrobolt or Thermophasic.

The D.I.E. Shockwave is available through the mystery box, completion of challenges, and via the following quest:

  1. Kill a Megaton and pick up the Keycard.
  2. Go to the 'Weapons Lab' and interact with the Weapon Locker and pick up the D.I.E. Remote Control.
  3. Go to the "Living Room" and interact with this Broken Wall to activate the "D.I.E.". Zombies will be sucked through the door for about 30 seconds. If the D.I.E. turns off without enough kills, it will go on cooldown for about 30 seconds. After which you can activate it again.
  4. Once enough zombies are sucked up, interact with the wall again, and the D.I.E. will shoot the door off.
  5. Once the door has been blown off, the D.I.E. Shockwave should be available for pickup.

    Nova-5

  1. Go to the "Mezzanine" and find the Empty Canister outside the map. Pull the canister toward you with the Alternate fire mode on the D.I.E.
  2. Pick up the Canister and bring it to the "Weapons Lab". Find the Cleaning Unit and place the Canister in it.
  3. Kill a Plaguehound near the canister.
  4. Pick up the canister and place it on the Nova-5 Weapon Crate and then melee it.
  5. You can now pick up the Nova-5 upgrade from the Weapon Crate.

    Cryo Emitter

  1. Go to the "Penthouse" and find the wooden crate on the ledge of the building and shoot it with the D.I.E. to knock it down. You can pick up the Flask from the crate.
  2. Have a Megaton shoot the tree in the 'Pond' with Fungus growing off it with its long range attack. If the Fungus is hit, it will turn purple.
  3. Place the Flask below the Fungus on the ground. Wait 60 Seconds for the flask to fill.
  4. Once filled, pick up the flask and interact with the Weapon Crate in the "Medical Bay".
  5. You can now pick up the Cryo-Emitter upgrade from the Weapon Crate.

    Elecrobolt

  1. Complete Steps 1-6 of the Main Quest
  2. Enter the Anomaly on the lowest level of the Particle Accelerator.
  3. Locate three Glowing Crystals and use the alt fire mode on the D.I.E. to collect the energy. Only one crystal's energy can be held at a time:
    • The Crash Site: Left of Juggernog
    • "Penthouse": Right of Wunderfizz
    • Pond: Right of Mystery Box
  4. Shoot the energy at the Weapon Crate in the Particle Accelerator.
  5. Once all 3 have been collected, you can pick up the upgrade from the Weapon Crate.

    Thermophasic

  1. Complete Steps 1-6 of the Main Quest.
  2. Enter the Anomaly in the 'Pond'.
  3. Go to the Crash Site and shoot/melee open the Fuse Box beside the Fast Travel Portal to pick up the Fuse.
  4. Go to the Weapons Lab and interact with the Plasma Cutter.
  5. Exit the Dark Aether and go to the 'Pond. Interact with the Plasma Cutter to get the Thermophasic Upgrade.

'Seal the Deal' Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 4/10, Est Completion Time - 40 Mins to 1 Hr, RNG Rating - 0/5

    Prerequisites

  1. Turn on Power and activate Pack-a-Punch

    2. Activate the Der Wechsler's Computer

  2. Collect the three Aetherscope parts in the Dark Aether. The parts spawn one at a time in a random order:
    • Particle Accelerator - near the Aether Tunnel
    • Yard - Beneath the concrete staircase near Rampage Inducer
    • Crash Site - near the cockpit on top of the plane debris
  3. Once you collect all three parts, exit the Dark Aether and build the Aetherscope at the workbench below Pack-a-Punch in the Particle Accelerator.
  4. Enter the Dark Aether Anomaly in the 'Medical Bay'. Collect Dr. Vogel's Diary, upstairs in the Medical Bay.
  5. You must interact with three Spectral Reflections in the following order. You must interact with one twice (activating the reflections then handing it the book) before moving to the next one. You don't need to wait for one to finish before talking to another.
    • Medical Bay - Behind Speed Cola
    • Control Room - In between Two Challenge Podiums
    • Particle Accelerator - In front of the Aetherscope Workbench
  6. Go back to the computer where you picked up the diary and interact with it.

    7. Creating Orlov

  7. You must now obtain all 4 D.I.E. Upgrades
  8. Once you have obtained all four upgrades, you must shoot coloured ports the corresponding D.I.E. Upgrade
    • Nova-5: Green Port
    • Cryo-emitter: Blue Port
    • Electrobolt: Yellow Port
    • Thermophasic: Orange Port
  9. Enter the Dark Aether via the Anomaly in the Medical Bay and watch the reflection on the walkway above Speed Cola
  10. Once the Relection ends, and you exit the Dark Aether, collect the Dark Aether Wrench on the ground where the reflection was.
  11. Go to the Yard and interact with the Tank 3 times. A zombie will then pop out of the top of the tank, kill it and throw an explosive at the open hatch. The Tank will fire if the explosive was aimed correctly.
  12. Go to The Crash Site and pick up the Decontamination Agent and carry it back to where you picked up the wrench in the Medical Bay and place it in the containment unit. Note: You cannot fast travel or mantle with the Decontamination Agent.
  13. Wait for a Megaton to Spawn. Split it by shooting it and then bring the two split Megatons under the large machine on the ceiling of the Medical Bay. They will each get sucked in one at a time.
  14. Once the two Bombers are in the machine, go back to the computer and interact with it to start a cutscene.

    15. Boss Fight

  15. Once the cutscene ends, go to the Omega Outpost (door near workbench coming from spawn) and enter the Anomaly. Near the M1911 wallbuy, there will be a Spectral Reflection, interact with it.

    16. Warning: Point of No Return. Once you do the next step, you will be put into the "boss fight"

  16. Once the Spectral Reflection is done, on a table by the reflection pick up the Orlov Family Photo.
  17. You will be teleported to the Particle Accelerator, where Orlov will talk to a bit before running to one of his objectives.
  18. You must protect Orlov from the zombies while he deactivates the Particle Accelerator. Orlov can't die, but he will stop working if there are zombies on him.
  19. There are three things Orlov has to do, the Magnetic Containment, Primary Coolant, and Fail-Safe.
  20. Once the Particle Accelerator is deactivated, a short dialogue will play, followed by a 90-second timer will start where the players need to make it thorough the facility avoiding lethal beams of electricity coming from the ground to get to the exfil chopper. There is only one path out which takes you through the Weapons Lab to the Control Room, out to the Tunnel then The Crash Site then down to the Pond. Board the exfil chopper to finish the Easter Egg.

'Alone' Song

  1. There are three tapes required to start the song
    • "Living Room": Inside the toppled refrigerator
    • Medical Bay: On a shelf near a wooden crate
    • Particle Accelerator: On top of machinery
  2. Once all three tapes are collected, the song will start

Coffin Dance (Jug EE)

  1. Turn on Power and Open Pack a Punch
  2. There are five orbs that need to be shot
    • Near the right-side door to Main Power
    • Within the barrier beneath the stairs
    • On an air duct near the door to Weapons Lab
    • Behind the grate on the Main Power wall
    • Behind the machinery to the right of the stairs, below the Armory
  3. Once all five orbs were shot, you will be teleported to the Dark Aether to watch a cutscene. Once the cutscene is over, you will be presented with a chest.

Free Legendary Weapon Upgrade

  1. Turn on Power and Open Pack a Punch
  2. Obtain a Pack-a-Punch'd Weapon

    3. For the Remainder of this Easter Egg you must be in the Dark Aether.

  3. Navigate to the Medical Bay shoot four red buttons lining the walls of the hallway with the barrier.
  4. Once all Four Buttons have turned green, a door on the left will begin to emit a green light.
  5. Shoot the button to the right of the glowing door to spawn an Orda's Hand from the door. The Hand will kill zombies near the door.
  6. Once the Hand kills 15 Zombies, your currently equipped weapon will be upgraded to Legendary.

Free Scorestreak or Jumpscare

  1. Turn on Power and Open Pack a Punch
  2. Obtain a Pack-a-Punch'd Weapon

    3. For the Remainder of this Easter Egg you must be in the Dark Aether.

  3. A Zombie can be found suspended in the air in each of the following five locations. The locations in which a Zombie will spawn is randomized each match and only one Zombie will be spawned at a time.
    • Particle Accelerator: Outside the barrier on the Weapons Lab side
    • Control Room: In the water beneath the catwalk to the Medical Bay
    • Pond: Near the trees left of the door to Tunnel
    • Crash Site: Near the tail of the crashed plane
    • Yard: Near the trees left of the door to "Living Room"

    If you want the Free Scorestreak, complete Step 4. Otherwise, if you want to see the Jumpscare, skip Step 4 and complete Step 5.

  4. Return to the Pond and locate the Zombie floating near the center of the Pond. Shoot it to reveal a Scorestreak which can be collected on the ground beneath where the Zombie was.
  5. Don't shoot the zombie, instead stand underneath it.

Free 1,500 Essence

  1. Turn on Power and Open Pack-a-Punch
  2. Obtain Dead Wire from Pack-a-Punch
  3. Rotate three satellites (pictured below) on towers across the map using Dead Wire-equipped weapons until they point toward the "Penthouse" antenna and emit a blue pulse.
    • Crash Site - Left of Stamin-Up
    • Crash Site - Right of Juggernog in the forest
    • Pond - Right of Quick Revive in the forest
  4. After a short animation, three Essence Canisters will appear near the wunderfizz available for pickup.
