Chrysalax Savager

The Chrysalax is available through the mystery box, completion of challenges, and via the following quest:
The Chrysalax can simply be Pack-A-Punched and does not require an Easter Egg to do so.

  1. Unlock Pack-a-Punch.
  2. Interact with the Button on the Observation Tower.
  3. To make the Chrysalax, the player needs to collect three items, Tempered Crystal Heart, Energetic Geode and Polymorphic Crystal Core. Each part can be obtained at anytime in any order.

    4. Tempered Crystal Heart

  4. Kill a Tormentor with the Flamethrower Scorestreak.
  5. When killed, the Tormentor will drop the Tempered Crystal Heart.

    6. Energetic Geode

  6. Wait for an Abomination or Tormentor to spawn.
  7. Locate and stand near a Dark Aether Crystal.
  8. Bait the Abomination into shooting the Crystal with its ranged lightning attack or have a Tormentor explode the Crystal.
  9. When hit by the lightning or exploded, the crystal will be turned into an Energetic Geode.

    10. Polymorphic Crystal Core

  10. Wait two rounds after pressing the Button on the Observation Tower.
  11. Navigate to Jim's Donuts on Main Street.
  12. Stand near the Crystal behind the counter. This will spawn three waves of enemies.
  13. The three waves consist of 5 Plaguehounds, three Tempests and finally two Mimics. Once the three waves have been killed, the Crystal become available for pickup.

    14. Crafting the Chrysalax

  14. Navigate to the Particle Accelerator and place the Crystals in to The Tub.
  15. Get 10 Melee Kills beside the Tub.
  16. Once 10 Zombies have been killed, the Chrysalax will become available for pickup.

'Pyrrhic Victory' Main Easter Egg Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 5/10, Est Completion Time - 20 to 40 Mins, RNG Rating - 0/5

    Prerequisites

  1. Unlock Pack-a-Punch.
  2. Interact with the Button on the Observation Tower.
  3. Obtain the Chrysalax. Note: The Chrysalax is required to fill the Fuel Tank. Since steps groups can be completed at anytime in any order, it's recommended to complete the other steps (Obtain the Monitoring Device or Obtain the Housing Unit) while crafting the Chrysalax.
  4. Similar to the Chrysalax, the player needs to collect three items to start the boss fight. The steps can be completed at anytime in any order.

    5. Obtain and Fill the Fuel Tank

  5. Navigate to Fuel Processing, each player must simultaneously interact with the Processing Tanks to activate them. The number of Processing Tanks that need to be activated depends on the number of players in the game.
  6. Once the Tanks have been activated, the area will be locked down and enemies will start to spawn. The player(s) must survive until the lockdown ends and the Fuel Tank falls to the floor.
  7. Pickup the Fuel Tank.
  8. Obtain the Chrysalax.
  9. Navigate to three large Aetherium Crystals (the locations below) and shoot the three orbs flying around the crystal (The best weapon for this is the Chrysalax's Gun Form because of its high damage). Once the orbs are destroyed, melee the Large Crystal with the Chrysalax to destroy it.
  10. Once destroyed, the Crystal will break into a Crystal Shard. After picking up the Crystal Shard, an Abomination will spawn nearby.
  11. Throw the Shard into the Abomination's mouth. After it swallows the Crystal, and its tail becomes red, kill it and collect the Catalyzed Crystal Shard that it drops.
    • Storage Zone 5 Rooftops
    • The Amplifier
    • Staging Area
  12. Repeat Steps 9-11 for the other two large Crystals.

    13. Obtain the Monitoring Device

  13. Obtain a Pack-a-Punched Weapon with Dead Wire
  14. Obtain from Zombie Drops or Buy an Arcade Token
  15. Navigate to the Arcade and electrocute a Zombie near the Grand Prix Arcade Machine.
  16. Interact with the Arcade Machine to spawn an ARC-XD. Drive the ARC-XD to the TV Store and locate the Loosened Vent behind the counter.
  17. Destroy the vent cover with the passive pulses from the ARC-XD and drive through the vent. Position the ARC-XD between the shelf and wall and detonate the ARC-XD.
  18. Go to the TV Store and pick up the Monitoring Device from the shelf.

    19. Obtain the Housing Unit

  19. Wait for an Abomination to spawn.
  20. Navigate to the Board Room (Wunderfizz Room) and locate the Housing Unit.
  21. Stand below the Housing Unit by the wall and bait the Abomination into charging this wall. Make sure to move out of the way of the Abomination, so it hits the wall.
  22. If the Abomination successfully hits the wall, the Housing Unit will fall to the floor and be available for pickup.

    23. Assemble and Escort the Aetherium Neutralizer

  23. Navigate to the Main Street and craft the Aetherium Neutralizer at the workbench by Tombstone.

    24. Warning: Point of No Return. Once you activate the Neutralizer, you will be put into the Boss Fight. There will be an opportunity to Buy Perks, Armour, Scorestreaks and Use PAP, but you can no longer explore the map or gain points.

  24. Interact with the Neutralizer to begin escorting it. The Neutralizer requires Catalyzed Crystal Shards for fuel. The Shards can be collected by destroying Orange Dark Aether Crystals.
  25. Escort the Neutralizer, refuelling when requested, till you reach the Containment Door.

    26. Warning: This is your last opportunity to Buy Perks, Armour, Scorestreaks and Use PAP. You cannot come back here during the boss fight.

    Forsaken Boss Fight:

  • To simplify the explanation of this boss fight, I'm going to split this into two parts: How to Attack and How The Forsaken Attacks.
  • How to Attack:
    1. The Forsaken has four armoured spots that need to be broken to be able to damage him. These spots can only be broken in the following order:
      Left Shoulder/Right Shoulder, Left Shoulder/Right Shoulder, Lower Torso and Top of Head.
    2. Once one of these spots has been broken, a weak spot will be revealed. The player needs to shoot these weak spots with either of the two laser cannons.
    3. To charge the cannons, you must charge Sam by killing zombies in the circle below her.
    4. Once she is charged, she will activate one of the cannons allowing you to shoot the Forsaken.
    5. Repeat Steps 1-4 for each of the four armoured/weak spots.
  • How The Forsaken Attacks:
    1. The Forsaken has four attacks: Lightning Slam, Blizzard, Energy Ball and Laser Eyes
    2. Lightning Slam: While he still has arms, The Forsaken can raise one of his arms and slam it on the ground which will produce electric shockwaves. Any players caught under the hand will be instantly downed. The shockwave ripples out from the Hand, doing less and less damage the further from the hand they get.
    3. Blizzard: If the players destroy the right arm first, the Forsaken will start attacking with the Blizzard attack. The Forsaken will raise his damaged arm and spawn two areas of frost that slow players within it.
    4. Energy Ball: If the players destroy the left arm first, the Forsaken will start attacking with the Energy Ball attack. The Forsaken will raise his damaged arm and produce tracking energy orbs that will do damage to any player in its radius.
    5. Once both arms are destroyed, the Forsaken will no longer use his Lightning Slam attack, however, he will still use the Energy Ball and Blizzard Attacks.
    6. Once both arms are destroyed, the Forsaken will shoot laser out of his eyes that tracks a player, doing damage if they're in the area.
  • Once the last weak spot has been shot and the health bar hits 0 and disappears, a narrative sequence will begin and the Forsaken will slowly damage all players till they go down. The players can down themselves (via explosives or letting the Zombies down them) before being killed by the Forsaken to skip some voicelines.
  • Once all players are downed and the voicelines are finished, a cutscene will start. After it is finished, an in-game cutscene will start followed by the final cutscene.

'Samantha's Ballad' Song

  1. Purchase PhD Slider (Tier V Required)
  2. You must land near the Scorched Part on the floor using the longer distance slide from PhD Slider. Repeat for all locations until an Aether Bunny spawns.
    • Slide from the Bar Rooftop to the scorched area near the TV Store.
    • Slide from the Video Store Rooftop to the scorched area near the barrier.
    • Slide from the Anytown Rooftops to the scorched area near the theatre.
  3. Once you collect the Aether Bunny, interact with the back of Bubby and place the Aether Bunny inside.
  4. A two-minute lockdown sequence will start where the player has to defend Bubby from Zombies, Tormentors and Krazny Soldats.
  5. Once the lockdown is finished, a large loot chest will spawn in front of Bubby, which contains various loot items along with a cassette tape.
  6. When the cassette tape is picked up, the song will start.

Pizza Delivery (Potential Free Ray Gun)

Note: The reward for this Easter Egg could be a Ray Gun, but it is not guaranteed.

  1. Equip Aether Shroud Field Upgrade (Skill Tier III). You can switch field upgrades mid-game by simply selecting a class that has that field upgrade equipped.
  2. Navigate to Beach Pizza and using Aether Shroud, Warp through the door.
  3. In this room is Ronald Raygun and a stack of pizza boxes. Warning: The Player can kill Ronald Raygun and if he's dead you can no longer finish this Easter Egg.
  4. Interact with the stack of pizza boxes to start a delivery. The Player must deliver the Pizza to one of the following four locations in 45 seconds:
    • Video Store 1F: Cardboard boxes near mannequin
    • Cinema: Bench near sitting mannequins
    • Bar 1F: Booth near mannequins
    • Burger Town: Booth near sitting mannequins
  5. After every order, return to Ronald Raygun to request a reward. This reward will be some random loot, which has the possibility to be a Ray Gun.
  6. There are four orders to be completed.

Free Perkaholic (All Perks for Free)

  1. Interact with the Button on the Observation Tower.
  2. Navigate to the Video Store and throw an explosive to the left of this shelf.
  3. The VHS should have fallen out from behind the shelf. Go prone and pick up the VHS.
  4. Navigate to the TV Store and insert the VHS into the VHS Player.
  5. After inserting the VHS, four TVs will light up Blue, Green, Red and Orange.
  6. The Player will need to play three rounds of Simon Says using these four TVs. Round 1 consists of a sequence of four, Round 2 is a sequence of eight and Round is a sequence of twelve. They will light up in a random order, and once the sequence is done, simply shoot the TVs in the same order as the sequence.
  7. If all three rounds are completed successfully, a single TV will light up green.
  8. Purchase Tombstone
  9. Go back to the TV Store and interact with the Green TV making it Red.
  10. Interacting with the TV will spawn a Spectral Entity that will occasionally corrupt the player's vision and will slowly follow and attempt to kill the player if they get too close.
  11. Quickly navigate to The Amplifier and go down and enter Tombstone Shadow.
  12. While in Tombstone Shadow, make your way to the Particle Accelerator and shoot the entity that is floating above it to split it into multiple copies.
  13. When the entity splits, shoot the one that has glowing eyes. Repeat until the Entity disappears in a puff of smoke.
  14. Once the entity is gone, the player can revive themselves and upon being revived they will be awarded with every perk.

Anytown Grand Prix (Free Points)

  1. Purchase PhD Slider
  2. Navigate to the Bar and go up the 2nd Floor.
  3. Locate the Anytown Grand Prix Arcade Machine and kill a Zombies using the Explosive Slide from PhD Slider near the Arcade Machine.
  4. All Players must obtain an Arcade Token either from Zombie Drops, buying from the Arcade Machine Token Dispenser or from the free Arcade Token Easter Egg.
  5. All Players must interact with the Arcade Machine to start the race.
  6. Using the ARC-XD, follow the arrows on the ground to complete the three laps. There are reward tiers based on how quickly the player finishes the race. Legendary - 5000 Points, Epic - 4000 Points, Rare - 3000 Points.

Free Pistol Upgrade

  1. Obtain a Pistol to upgrade
  2. Navigate to Anytown West and pick up the blueprint from on top of the plants in front of the arcade.
  3. Run over to the window of the American Records and Tapes Store.
  4. The player must then, using the Pistol, shoot 18 targets that appear on both levels of the store.
  5. If all 18 targets were shot, the Pistol should be instantly upgrade rarity.

Free Sniper Upgrade

  1. Obtain a Sniper to upgrade
  2. Navigate to Video Store Rooftop and pick up the blueprint from on top of the air conditioning unit.
  3. The player must then, using the Sniper, shoot 15 targets that appear across the street.
  4. If all 15 targets were shot, the Sniper should instantly upgrade rarity.

Free SMG Rarity Upgrade

  1. Obtain a SMG to upgrade
  2. Navigate to Main Street and open the door to Beach Pizza. Pick up the blueprint from on top of the cinder blocks.
  3. The player must then, using the SMG, shoot targets in three different areas.
  4. There are six targets in Beach Pizza.
  5. There are five targets in Storage Zone 10.
  6. There are five targets in Storage Zone 10 Rooftops.
  7. If all 16 targets were shot, the SMG should be instantly upgrade rarity.

Free Arcade Token

  1. Simply Melee the Arcade Machine Token Dispenser.
  2. It will dispense an Arcade Token. This can be done only once per match.
